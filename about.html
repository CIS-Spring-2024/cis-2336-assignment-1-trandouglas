About Us

Owner & Founder

Douglas Tran is passionate about creating delicious and nutritious meals for the community. With over 10 years of experience in the culinary industry, he established UOH Sugarland Cafeteria with the aim of providing fresh, homemade dishes using locally sourced ingredients.

Douglas is planning to create and give more to his community for the better future.
